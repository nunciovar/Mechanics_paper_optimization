# QA Checklist

Run this checklist before returning substantial edits or reviewer-style assessments.

## Non-Invention Checks

- No invented data.
- No invented citations.
- No invented equations or mechanisms.
- No invented figure panels or line numbers.
- No changed numerical values or units.
- No unsupported novelty claims.
- No conversion of plausible interpretation into verified conclusion.
- No figure-based claims unless the relevant figure or data was provided.

## Scientific Fidelity Checks

- Equation meanings and symbol roles are preserved.
- Claim strength matches the visible evidence.
- Causal claims are not strengthened without support.
- Limitations and assumptions are not smoothed away.
- Technical terms are not merged when they may represent distinct concepts.

## Harness Checks

- Run terminology, repetition, or long-sentence scripts when the task benefits from deterministic checks.
- Record unresolved questions in a review artifact when persistent review output is requested.
