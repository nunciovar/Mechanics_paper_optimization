## Damage And Fracture Terminology

| Term | Intended scale | Use when | Avoid when |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| damage field | field variable or contour/cloud map | describing plotted damage values | referring to a fully formed crack unless supported |
| damage evolution | loading-process description | describing how damage changes during loading | naming a static field plot |
| bond failure | PD bond-level event | describing broken or failed bonds | describing macroscopic fracture alone |
| fracture process | broader failure process | discussing macroscopic crack or process-zone behavior | replacing specific bond/damage terms |
